Weaving Stories, Creating Legacies

For over two decades, RugSpace has been at the forefront of bringing authentic Indian craftsmanship to contemporary homes worldwide.

Our Story

It all began in 2001 when founder Rajesh Khanna discovered a centuries-old weaving technique in a small village near Jaipur. What started as fascination became a mission to preserve traditional artistry while making it relevant for today's interiors.

From our humble beginnings with just five artisan families, RugSpace has grown to support over 200 skilled craftspeople across Rajasthan, Kashmir, and West Bengal. Each rug tells a story of dedication, where ancient techniques meet contemporary design sensibilities.

Today, we're proud to be India's leading curator of premium handcrafted rugs, serving discerning customers in over 25 countries while maintaining our commitment to ethical practices and exceptional quality.

Our Craft Process

From concept to your home, every step is carefully orchestrated

01

Design & Planning

Our creative team works closely with artisans to develop patterns that blend traditional motifs with contemporary aesthetics, creating unique designs for modern spaces.

02

Material Sourcing

We carefully select the finest natural materials - premium wool from Kashmir, silk from Varanasi, and organic cotton - ensuring sustainability and quality in every fiber.

Master artisans bring designs to life using time-honored techniques. Each rug takes weeks to months to complete, depending on size and complexity of the pattern.
